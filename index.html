Reasons why

I created a website about the Day of the Dead festival as a way to share my passion for this colorful and meaningful celebration. Initially, I had planned to create a website about Alexander the Great's mother, but after not finding any pictures online,

I became stressed and started to look for other topics to explore. As I thought about what inspired me,

I remembered my travels to Mexico over a decade ago and the vivid memories of the Day of the Dead celebrations. The idea stuck, and I decided to create a website to share the history, traditions, and beauty of this unique and fascinating festival.

Documentary

This documentary explores the history and evolution of the Mexican Day of the Dead, from its pagan origins to its current multi-faceted observances.

Through the perspectives of artist Betsabee Romero,curator Laura Osorio Sunnucks, and paleobiologist Omar Regalado Fernandez, viewers are introduced to the ways in which the living remember and honor the dead through cooking,eating, drinking, music-making, and grave decoration.

The documentary highlights the importance of the "spirit of things" and the love that is shared in these rituals.
